#hackPHX Arduino

Friday

We'll see you this Friday, March 1st, from 7pm-~10pm for opening ceremonies, team matching, and secret ingredient unveiling. Note you MUST attend this session or we'll be forced to release your ticket. It'll be packed in downtown Mesa, with Main Street closed for Motorcycles on Main, so be prepared to spend a little time parking. You'll spend happy hour and as long as you need planning out what you're hoping to do the following day, but you won't take anything withyou. Go home and get some sleep!

Saturday

On Saturday, we'll see you bright and early by 9:30am, ready to hack by 10am. Bring your laptop, Arduino toolkit, and anything else you'll need to win. You'll have the hackerspace's resources to make something amazing. Thanks to our sponsors, lunch and dinner will be catered so don't worry about that. You can go out, spend a small budget at Radio Shack, Walmart, on Craigstlist, etc, to bolster your creation. We'll check code and assets into Github by ~8pm and followi up with demos, judging, and prizes!

Prizes

After demos the judges will pick a team that impressed them the most. That could mean literally anything except breaking our limited rules list. Then, you, the Jury, will decide which team we robbed based on WHATEVER criteria you choose to make sure they're rewarded as well!

Because of the amazing support of our sponsors we're giving away Google Nexii courtesy of Microchip & Iced Dev and amazing test equipment from Elenco and Seeed Studio!

Rules

  • Must consent to video and photos throughout the event.
  • Must incorporate the ingredient.
  • Must use Arduino (this can mean Arduino clones using Arduino code or Arduino TM boards running straight Atmel code).
  • Budget must be limited, proof with receipts, price matching, etc. All contingent upon judge approval.
  • Must put source code, hardware, BOM, etc on Github day of event when we call contest to a close.
  • Must not be a dick.
Thats it!

So make your Body and Mind ready, and please if you can't make it let us know so we can open your ticket for some other lucky hacker.

Please see the FAQ for other questions or email us at info@hackphx.com -- and definitely let us know if you have dietary needs.
